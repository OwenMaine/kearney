The Hands
Behind the Hardware.

From Spark
To Staple

Founded in 1985 by the Kearney family, our foundry began with a simple goal: to provide Fresno's builders and artisans with uncompromising quality. Over three decades, we've grown from a small-scale workshop into a community pillar, weathering industry changes by holding fast to the principles of good material, honest work, and strong relationships.

Our mission is to empower creation. We believe in providing not just the materials, but the support and knowledge to bring our customers' visions to life. Quality is our creed, customer service is our promise, and innovation is our path forward. We are committed to being the most reliable partner in your project's success.

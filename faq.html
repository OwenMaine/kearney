Shipping & Pickup

Yes, we offer local delivery for orders that meet a minimum size requirement. Delivery fees and schedules vary based on distance and order size. Please call us to arrange a delivery.

Returns & Warranty

Unused, uncut, and undamaged standard stock materials can be returned within 30 days of purchase with a valid receipt for a full refund or exchange. A small restocking fee may apply.

Due to their bespoke nature, all custom-fabricated parts are final sale and cannot be returned. We guarantee our workmanship and will address any manufacturing defects or deviations from the approved design specifications.
